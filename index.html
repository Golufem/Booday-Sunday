Meet the man of the People SUNDAY JEREMIAH ADEYEMO

SUNDAY JEREMIAH ADEYEMO, popularly known as BODA SUNDAY ADEYEMO is a native of Ogbomoso, Oyo state. He is a startup founder and a philanthropist.

BODA SUNDAY ADEYEMO had his primary education at Model Primary school, Suleja, Niger state. He had his secondary school education at Bishop James Yisa Memorial Secondary school, Suleja, Niger state. He then proceeded to Ladoke Akintola University of Technology (LAUTECH), Ogbomoso where he studied Etectrical Electronics Engineering (B.Tech).

As a true visionary, BODA SUNDAY ADEYEMO currently leads Trinity Innovations, a growing business that has provided jobs for over 20 people. He also Leads BODA SUNDAY ADEYEMO FOUNDATION (BSA), a foundation situated in Ogbomoso, Oyo state, which has embarked on several projects like: Construction of Drainage, Construction of Okada tent in various locations across Ogbomoso, Renovation of abandoned borehole across Ogbomoso South LG, amongst many others.

It is pertinent to note that SUNDAY JEREMIAH ADEYEMO (aka BODA SUNDAY ADEYEMO) has no affiliation with Mr Sunday Igboho of Igboho, Oyo state.
